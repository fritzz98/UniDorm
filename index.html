UniDorm

UniDorm is the app that unifies all student dormitories based in a particular city. The app will contain all important information
about every dorm and will be designed so that is easy to use. Students will be able to enter a variety of preferences when it comes
to choosing the perfect place for living. Based on the selected choices such as University location, preferably price range, type
of the room etc. the app will offer a few of the best options. After getting accepted by the student dorm, the students will have
access to chat rooms where they can connect with other students.
