About Us

As students who came to live and study in another country, we have faced up different issues while finding the perfect accommodation for ourselves.
Also, it is difficult to integrate into the city where the foreign language is spoken and when one does not have any acquaintances.


Our vision is to create a network of students and student dormitories as well as the network between students themselves. It is our goal to get 80% of the target cities student dorms to use our app in the first 3 years.


Our mission is to create such an app that will make the beginning of the study for abroad students much easier.
UniDorm is the app that unifies all student dormitories based in a particular city.
The app will contain all important information about every dorm and will be designed so that is easy to use.
Students will be able to enter a variety of preferences when it comes to choosing the perfect place for living.
Based on the selected choices such as University location, preferably price range, type of the room etc. the app will offer a few of the best options.
After getting accepted by the student dorm, the students will have access to chat rooms where they can connect with other students.
